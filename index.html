Shut The Box Rules

  • Shut the Box is a traditional, fast-paced dice game where players aim to cover, or shut, numbered tiles (1–9) based on dice rolls.
  • Players roll two dices, adding the total, and flip down tiles that match that sum, either individually or in combination.
  • Player can only close up to 2 tiles maximum
  • E.g., if 2 dices(4 & 5), sum of 9 is rolled, you can choose to close either 4 or 5, both or 9.
  • The objective is to shut all tiles to win, player loses when no valid moves are possible after a roll. The final score is based on the sum of remaining tiles.
